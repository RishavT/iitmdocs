# Misc FAQs (Laptop/Device, VPN, Language, Campus Facilities, International/Logistics, Scholarships/Financial, Student ID/Email, Office/Campus Visit/Payments, System Compatibility/Misc, Learning/Support, GenAI/Misc Academic, Payments edge cases)



**Question**: What if I don't have money to buy a laptop?
**Answer**: IIT Madras BS programme does not provide laptops. Learners must arrange their own laptop or desktop.



**Question**: Will IITM provide laptop for scholarship students?
**Answer**: No. Laptops are not provided.



**Question**: How to get VPN access from IITM?
**Answer**: IIT Madras does not provide VPN access.



**Question**: What is VPN service? How will it help?
**Answer**: VPN access is not provided by IIT Madras.



**Question**: Is the class in Hindi or English?
**Answer**: All classes and materials are in English.



**Question**: Does the degree offer a hostel facility?
**Answer**: No. Hostel facilities are not provided.



**Question**: Can students access the central library?
**Answer**: No. Facilities like hostel, central library, sports complex, and clinic are not available.



**Question**: Can I enter IIT Madras campus?
**Answer**: BS students with a valid student ID may visit the campus for a day, but cannot access campus facilities.



**Question**: Can I do projects outside India?
**Answer**: Yes. The programme is fully online and can be completed from anywhere.



**Question**: I am an abroad student, can I do this degree?
**Answer**: Yes. The programme is open to learners worldwide.



**Question**: Exam timing for outside-India students
**Answer**: Exams follow IST timings.



**Question**: Are external scholarships available?
**Answer**: External scholarships are not regularly provided.



**Question**: Will I get a student ID card after qualifier?
**Answer**: Yes, after registering for Foundation level courses.



**Question**: When will I get student ID and email?
**Answer**: Student email is issued after enrolling in Foundation level, usually a few weeks later.



**Question**: Can I submit application at IITM BS office?
**Answer**: No. Applications must be submitted online only.



**Question**: Should I come offline to campus to make payment?
**Answer**: No. Payments are online only.



**Question**: Can I come to campus to make payment?
**Answer**: No. Payments are online only.



**Question**: What is SCT?
**Answer**: SCT is a System Compatibility Test, mandatory for remote-proctored exams.



**Question**: Student handbook for September 2025
**Answer**: Handbook is continuously updated; latest version contains ongoing updates.



**Question**: Will I get recorded lectures?
**Answer**: Yes. All lectures are pre-recorded and accessible anytime.



**Question**: How to clear doubts in subjects?
**Answer**: Via discussion forums, live sessions, study groups, resources, and instructor email.



**Question**: What is Gen AI theory and project?
**Answer**: Explanation of GenAI theory concepts and hands-on project work.
