# Misc FAQs



**Question**: What if I don't have money to buy a laptop?
**Answer**: IIT Madras BS programme does not provide laptops. Learners must arrange their own laptop or desktop.



**Question**: Will IITM provide laptop for scholarship students?
**Answer**: No. Laptops are not provided.



**Question**: How to get VPN access from IITM?
**Answer**: IIT Madras does not provide VPN access.



**Question**: What is VPN service? How will it help?
**Answer**: VPN access is not provided by IIT Madras.



**Question**: Is the class in Hindi or English?
**Answer**: All classes and materials are in English.



**Question**: Does the degree offer a hostel facility?
**Answer**: No. Hostel facilities are not provided.



**Question**: Can students access the central library?
**Answer**: No. Facilities like hostel, central library, sports complex, and clinic are not available.



**Question**: Can I enter IIT Madras campus?
**Answer**: BS students with a valid student ID may visit the campus for a day, but cannot access campus facilities.



**Question**: Can I do projects outside India?
**Answer**: Yes. The programme is fully online and can be completed from anywhere.



**Question**: I am an abroad student, can I do this degree?
**Answer**: Yes. The programme is open to learners worldwide.
For international student details, visit: https://study.iitm.ac.in/ds/admissions.html#AD9



**Question**: Exam timing for outside-India students
**Answer**: Exams follow IST timings.



**Question**: Are external scholarships available?
**Answer**: External scholarships are not regularly provided.



**Question**: Will I get a student ID card after qualifier?
**Answer**: Yes, after registering for Foundation level courses.



**Question**: When will I get student ID and email?
**Answer**: Student email is issued after enrolling in Foundation level, usually a few weeks later.



**Question**: Can I submit application at IITM BS office?
**Answer**: No. Applications must be submitted online only.



**Question**: Should I come offline to campus to make payment?
**Answer**: No. Payments are online only.



**Question**: Can I come to campus to make payment?
**Answer**: No. Payments are online only.



**Question**: What is SCT?
**Answer**: SCT is a System Compatibility Test, mandatory for remote-proctored exams.
All students must mandatorily attend the SCT and ensure their system conforms to the requirements. Dates for SCT are communicated by the Admin team via email.



**Question**: Student handbook for September 2025
**Answer**: Handbook is continuously updated; latest version contains ongoing updates.



**Question**: Will I get recorded lectures?
**Answer**: Yes. All lectures are pre-recorded and accessible anytime.



**Question**: How to clear doubts in subjects?
**Answer**: Via discussion forums, live sessions, study groups, resources, and instructor email.



**Question**: What is Gen AI theory and project?
**Answer**: Gen AI theory covers the conceptual foundations of generative artificial intelligence, including model architectures, fundamentals of learning, and ethics and responsible AI.
Gen AI projects involve hands-on practice where learners apply what they have learned, such as building and fine-tuning models and working on applied AI tasks.



**Question**: How can I become a topper in the BS programme?
**Answer**: To be among the toppers, learners must consistently perform well across exams and assignments. The programme recognises top performers as follows:

* Highest Scores — students with the highest CGPA at each level
* Certificates of Academic Distinction — CGPA > 9.5
* Certificates of Merit — CGPA > 9.0

These recognitions are announced and awarded at the annual Paradox student fest.



**Question**: Are there physical practical classes or labs?
**Answer**: No.
The IIT Madras BS programme is fully online. There are no physical practical classes or labs. All sessions and coursework are accessible online only.



**Question**: Will those enrolled to this program have access to IIT Madras campus facilities?
**Answer**: Due to limitations of campus facilities and students being spread out geographically, learners enrolled in the program will not have access to IITM campus facilities.



**Question**: What is the language of instruction for these courses? Are they available in other regional languages?
**Answer**: All our program courses are taught in English. Hence, we expect a minimum proficiency in English language to participate in the program.



**Question**: What are the technological requirements for this program?
**Answer**: Access to good internet connection as well as a laptop / desktop device will be a key requirement to learn effectively from our courses. Familiarity with Google tools would be an advantage.



**Question**: Will the classes be taught live? Will there be any interaction?
**Answer**: No. Pre-recorded lessons and assignments will be made available on our portal on a weekly basis. Learners can learn from the content released each week at their own pace, but will be required to submit the weekly assignments online within stipulated deadlines.

One or two LIVE sessions per course may be conducted to clear doubts and interact with the course instructor and course support team.



**Question**: How / where do I ask questions or doubts related to the course content of the program?
**Answer**: Each course includes a dedicated discussion forum where learners can post course-related questions and interact with the course instructor or the course support team.

Students can also attend the live interaction sessions to get their queries addressed by the instructors.

For all questions not related to the course, you may write to support@study.iitm.ac.in



**Question**: Are there any sample video content or assignments?
**Answer**: As a sample, Week 1 content videos have been made available for the first four Foundational Level courses for you to try and learn from. We recommend you to check out these lectures and try the sample assignment we have put out for each course. The links to Week 1 Content and Assignment pages can be accessed from the Foundational Level courses section in the Academics page.



**Question**: Will IIT Madras provide the course material for the program in hard copy through courier (like IGNOU)?
**Answer**: No, the course content will be provided only in online mode so you can watch them anytime, anywhere. There will be no hard copy provided. Depending on the course, learners may be recommended reference books / material that they may buy separately.



**Question**: Can I change my email ID in the Foundational Level? Will we get any official student email ID?
**Answer**: The personal email ID you applied for the program with cannot be changed. This email ID is used to access the whole Qualifier Process and the Foundation Level Course registration Form.

Once all the documents uploaded in the foundation level course registration form are approved, each registered learner will be assigned a roll number and a corresponding official IIT Madras BS in Data Science and Applications student email ID. After that, all further communication and course access will be through the official student email ID.



**Question**: Will I be given a new portal to access courses at Foundational Level?
**Answer**: No, the current portal will be used for all the Levels. You will be given a new official IIT Madras BS in Data Science and Applications student email ID to access the portal.



**Question**: Will I be issued an ID card?
**Answer**: An ID card in electronic format will be provided, subject to conditions on use of ID card.
