# Refund Policy

## Application Fee - Non-Refundable
The Qualifier exam application fee is NON-REFUNDABLE under ANY circumstances.

This includes:
- Unable to write the exam
- Absent from exam
- Failed the exam
- Changed mind about joining
- Medical emergencies
- Personal reasons

## Reattempt Fee - Non-Refundable
The reattempt fee is also non-refundable.

## Course Fee Refunds
Course registration fees follow program refund policy. Contact support@study.iitm.ac.in for specific cases.

## Before Paying
Consider the following before registering:
- Are you sure about joining?
- Can you commit to the schedule?
- Do you have time for coursework?
- Can you attend exams at selected cities?

## Fee Structure Summary
| Fee Type | Amount | Refundable |
|----------|--------|------------|
| Application Fee (General/OBC) | Rs 4000 | No |
| Application Fee (SC/ST/PwD) | Rs 2000 | No |
| Reattempt Fee (General/OBC) | Rs 2000 | No |
| Reattempt Fee (SC/ST/PwD) | Rs 1000 | No |

## Getting Money Back
You will NOT get your money back if:
- You don't want the course anymore
- You can't write the exam
- You are absent
- You fail

# FAQs

**Question**: How long is each term?
**Answer**: Each term in the IIT Madras BS program lasts 16 weeks.



**Question**: When will exams be conducted in a term?
**Answer**: Exams in a term include:

* Quizzes: Usually two per term, with dates announced at the beginning of the term
* End-term exam: Conducted at the end of the term

Exact dates are published in the academic calendar: https://study.iitm.ac.in/ds/academic_calendar.html



**Question**: Is the academic schedule available?

**Answer**: Yes.
The academic schedule and important dates are available on the official IIT Madras BS program website under the Admissions section: https://study.iitm.ac.in/ds/admissions.html#AD0



**Question**: What is OPPE?

**Answer**: OPPE (Online Proctored Programming Exam) is conducted for programming-based courses that require learners to write and execute code.



**Question**: Which courses have OPPE?

**Answer**: OPPE is applicable only to selected programming courses, including:

Foundation level

* Introduction to Python Programming

Diploma level

* DBMS
* PDSA (Programming, Data Structures & Algorithms)
* Java
* System Commands / Linux
* Machine Learning Practice

Degree level

* C Programming
* Introduction to Big Data


**Question**: What happens if I get an Incomplete (I) grade?

**Answer**: Learners with an Incomplete (I) grade may:

* Register for a Make-Up Examination in the immediately subsequent term

If they do not register for the Make-Up Exam in the next term:

* They must repeat the entire course as a full course in a later term



**Question**: Will I receive certificates or marksheets?

**Answer**: Certificate formats and samples are available on the official website: https://study.iitm.ac.in/ds/academics.html#AC17
Learners are advised to refer to the website for the latest certificate templates.



**Question**: Is there an attendance policy for this program?
**Answer**: There is no daily attendance, but once you register for the courses, submission of weekly assignment is taken as an attendance indicator. Minimum required scores in weekly assignments of a course will determine if a learner will be allowed to write the end term exam for that course or not.



**Question**: Can I take my exams from home?
**Answer**: No. Every term will have quizzes and an end term exam for each course. All quizzes and end term exams will be in-person, invigilated exams at designated centres across the country. You need to travel to the exam centre and take these exams.



**Question**: What is a quiz?
**Answer**: A quiz is similar or equivalent to a monthly test in schools and colleges. Marks obtained in quizzes count towards the total score obtained in the course. All quizzes will be in-person, invigilated exams at designated centres across the country.



**Question**: Are the exam dates flexible?
**Answer**: No, the exam dates are not flexible. The quiz and end term exam dates are fixed for all learners taking the same course in a term. We try our best to schedule all exams during the weekends though it may not be possible for every exam.



**Question**: What if I want to request for a city not listed in the current list of exam cities?
**Answer**: If the city of your choice is not in our current exam cities list, please send an email to support@study.iitm.ac.in. We will consider your request, but there is no guarantee that we will add it.



**Question**: Will there be multiple exam centres within an exam city? How many?
**Answer**: There may be more than one exam centre in any exam city. The count depends on the number of learners in each city and availability of centres on given date with our exam partner.



**Question**: When will the term start? What is the timeline?
**Answer**: Learners who clear the Qualifier Exam will be expected to register for the program right after their results are announced. In the first term alone, classes start (with week 5) immediately after the completion of week 4 in the qualifier phase with no gap. Please check the Important Dates in the Admissions page to get to know the timeline.



**Question**: What is the eligibility to write the End Term Exam for a course?
**Answer**: To be eligible to write the End Term Exam for a course, it is mandatory for the learner to have (i) obtained in the course an Average Assignment Score >= 40/100 AND (ii) appeared for at least one out of the 2 in-person Quizzes.

Learners who are not eligible to write a specific course's End Term Exam will not be issued hall ticket for the same. They will have to repeat the entire course including assignments and quizzes in a later term.



**Question**: Is it mandatory to take the proctored in-person Quizzes?
**Answer**: It is mandatory to attempt at least one of the 2 in-person quizzes in a term to be eligible to appear for the End Term Exams.



**Question**: Will Qualifier phase assignment scores be included while calculating the eligibility for the End Term?
**Answer**: The Qualifier Exam Score will be counted as Quiz 1 Score for the courses registered in the Foundation level in the same term of the qualifier exam.
For courses that you register for in a later term, you will be required to repeat the assignments completed in the Qualifier phase.



**Question**: What will happen if I am absent for an End Term Exam?
**Answer**: Learner has two options:

1. Register for the course in the subsequent term with the option of taking the End Term Exam alone and by paying a reduced fee (marks of assignments and quizzes will be carried over)
2. Repeat the entire course (including assignments and quizzes) by paying the full course fee.



**Question**: How can I change the exam city within India?
**Answer**: Each exam has a deadline before which change of exam city is allowed. Option to change the exam city will be available till each deadline in the student Dashboard, in the "Exam Cities and Hall Tickets" page.
