# International Students Guide

## Eligibility
Yes, international students from any country (Bangladesh, Sri Lanka, Nepal, USA, UK, etc.) can enroll in the IIT Madras BS program.

## Exam Mode for International Students
- Exams conducted online with live remote proctoring
- No need to travel to India for exams
- Exams from your residence

## Exam Timing
- 9 AM IST and 2 PM IST slots
- Synced with India Standard Time
- Consider time zone difference when planning

## Additional Fees
International students pay:
- Same tuition/course fees as Indian students
- Additional Rs 2000 per subject as exam facilitation fee

## Required Documents for International Students
### Residence Proof (any one):
- Residence Card
- Driving License with address
- Bank Statement with address (can mask transactions)
- Utility Bill (phone/electricity)

### ID Proof:
- Recent visa page with date stamp, OR
- Citizenship document

## Payment Issues
If facing payment errors from outside India:
- Contact support@study.iitm.ac.in
- Try different payment methods
- Provide transaction details if payment fails

## Contact for International Students
ge@study.iitm.ac.in (Global Entry support)

## Exam City Information for International Students
Check availability at: https://study.iitm.ac.in/ds/academics.html#AC9
