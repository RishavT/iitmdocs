Q129: How many terms are there in a year? What is the duration of each term?
Answer: There are 3 terms per year: January term, May term, and September term. The qualifier phase is 4 weeks, followed by the regular term coursework. Each regular term spans approximately 3-4 months including exams.

Q130: How do I register for foundation level courses after passing the qualifier?
Answer: After passing the qualifier: 1) Log in to the student portal 2) Select up to 4 Foundation courses based on your score 3) Upload Class 12 documentation if needed 4) Choose 2 exam cities 5) Pay fees (~Rs 6000 per course) 6) Submit before deadline (typically 1-4 days after results).

Q131: Can I take a break after clearing the qualifier exam? How long is my result valid?
Answer: Yes, you can defer joining after clearing the qualifier. Your qualifier result is valid for 3 terms (approximately 1 year) if you have completed Class 12. You can register for foundation courses in any of these 3 terms.

Q132: Can I take Math 2 if I have U grade in Math 1? Can I register for both simultaneously?
Answer: No. Math 1 is a prerequisite for Math 2. You must clear Math 1 first before registering for Math 2. You cannot take both courses simultaneously if you have a U grade in Math 1.
