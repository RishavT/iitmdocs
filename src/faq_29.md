Q127: If i have doubts in the subject, how do i get them cleared?
Answer: If you have doubts in your subjects while enrolled in the IIT Madras BS program, here are some ways to get them cleared:

Discussion Forums: Participate in the course's online discussion forums. Engaging with peers and instructors can help clarify your doubts.

Live sessions : Students who have doubts in the courses can attend the Live interaction online sessions. The dates on which the sessions are conducted can be seen in the student calendar while you log in to the portal.
Study Groups: Form or join study groups with fellow students. Collaborating with others can provide different perspectives and solutions to your doubts.

Course Resources: Utilize any additional resources provided in the course, such as recorded lectures, reading materials, and supplementary exercises.

Email Support: If your doubts are specific and require detailed explanations, consider emailing your instructor directly for assistance.
