# Complete Fee Structure - IIT Madras BS Program

## Qualifier Exam Application Fee
| Category | Application Fee |
|----------|-----------------|
| General / OBC | Rs 4000 |
| SC / ST / PwD (40%+ disability) | Rs 2000 |
| SC/ST who is also PwD | Rs 1000 |

## Qualifier Reattempt Fee (within same term)
| Category | Reattempt Fee |
|----------|---------------|
| General / OBC | Rs 2000 |
| SC / ST / PwD | Rs 1000 |
| SC/ST + PwD | Rs 500 |

## Course Fees (Foundation Level onwards)
- Per course fee: Rs 6000 approximately
- Foundation level (8 courses): Rs 48,000

## Total Program Fees by Level
| Level | Total Credits | Total Fees (INR) |
|-------|---------------|------------------|
| Foundation only | 32 | Rs 48,000 |
| Foundation + One Diploma | 59 | Rs 1,29,000 |
| Foundation + Two Diplomas | 86 | Rs 2,10,000 |
| BSc Degree | 114 | Rs 2,86,000 - 3,10,000 |
| BS Degree | 142 | Rs 3,86,000 - 4,50,000 |
| BS + PG Diploma in AI & ML | 162 | Rs 4,86,000 - 5,90,000 |
| BS + MTech in AI & ML | 182 | Rs 6,86,000 - 7,90,000 |

## Payment Information
- All fees are paid online through the student portal
- Application fee is non-refundable under any circumstances
- Reattempt fee is also non-refundable
- No option to pay fees in person at campus
- For payment issues contact: support@study.iitm.ac.in

## International Students
Additional exam facilitation fee: Rs 2000 per subject

## Fee Waiver
Fee waivers are available for eligible categories. Contact support for details about fee waiver documentation requirements.
