# Qualifier Results Communication

## Communication Channels

All official communication related to the Qualifier Process including assignment deadlines, examination schedules, hall ticket release, and publication of results will be shared with learners through the following channels:

1. **Email:** The registered email address of the learner
2. **WhatsApp:** Notifications sent to the registered mobile number
3. **Portal:** Announcements displayed on the student portal

## Important Recommendations

- Learners are advised to check their registered email frequently
- Ensure that WhatsApp notifications remain enabled
- This helps ensure no important information or deadline is missed

## After Qualifying

- Learners who qualify will be permitted to proceed with further registration
- An Admission Letter will be generated for each eligible candidate

## Results Display

- The qualifier exam marks will be displayed within the login on the portal
- Email and WhatsApp alerts will be sent to candidates about the results
