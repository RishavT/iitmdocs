# Qualifier Exam Passing Criteria

In the qualifier exam comprising 4 subjects, the learner should obtain marks >= individual subject cut-off as shown below. The total of all subject marks should be greater than or equal to the total cut-off.

## Cutoff Marks by Category

| Category | Min. Req. Qualifier Exam Score in Each Course | Min. Req. Average Qualifier Exam Score |
|----------|-----------------------------------------------|---------------------------------------|
| General Learner | 40% | 50% |
| SC / ST / PwD with 40% disability | 30% | 40% |
| PwD with 40% disability & SC / ST | 30% | 40% |
| OBC-NCL / EWS | 35% | 45% |

## How to Pass

To pass the qualifier exam, you must:
1. Score at least the minimum required percentage in EACH of the 4 subjects (as per your category)
2. Have an average score across all 4 subjects that meets or exceeds the minimum required average (as per your category)

## Example

For a General category learner:
- Must score at least 40% in each of the 4 subjects
- Must have an average of at least 50% across all 4 subjects

## Result Communication

- The qualifier exam marks will be displayed within the login on the portal
- An email and WhatsApp alert will be sent to the candidates about the results

# Assignment Grading and Category-wise Cutoffs

## Assignment Grading
- Each assignment will be graded out of 100
- Any assignment that is not attempted will be marked as 0

## Minimum Average Assignment Score by Category

Only those who get the minimum required average assignment scores in all four courses will be allowed to appear for the Qualifier Exam.

| Category | Minimum Average Assignment Score Required in Each Course |
|----------|----------------------------------------------------------|
| General Learner | 40% |
| SC / ST / PwD with 40% disability | 30% |
| PwD with 40% disability & SC / ST | 30% |
| OBC-NCL / EWS | 35% |

## Important Notes
- Relaxations in pass criteria indicated for various categories of learners are applicable ONLY for the qualifier process
- There will be no relaxations in terms of grades or pass criteria once registered into the program
- Hall tickets will be released only for candidates eligible to take the in-person proctored Qualifier Exam

# Category-wise Cutoffs and Relaxations

## Qualifier Exam Passing Criteria by Category

### General Category
- Minimum per subject: 40%

### OBC-NCL / EWS Category
- Minimum per subject: 35%
- Minimum overall average: 45%

### SC / ST / PwD (40%+ disability)

## Eligibility Criteria for Qualifier Exam

### First Attempt Eligibility
- If the average of the first 2 weeks' assignment scores is ≥ 40/100 (or as per the cutoff for each category of students) in each of the 4 subjects, the students will be eligible to write the qualifier exam in the first attempt within the term.

### Second Attempt Eligibility
- For students who do not qualify after the week 2 assignments, the average of the best 2 out of the first 3 weeks' assignment scores will be considered.
- If this score is ≥ 40/100 (or as per the cutoff for each category of students) in each of the 4 subjects, the students will become eligible to write the qualifier exam in the second attempt within the term.
- Reattempts within the term will be given suitably.
