# Working Professionals and Parallel Study

## Can Working Professionals Apply?
Yes. Working professionals can apply for the IIT Madras BS program. No special approval needed if you meet basic eligibility (Class 12 passed).

## Pursuing Another Degree
Yes, you can pursue the IITM BS program alongside another degree or job. The program is designed to be flexible.

## Time Commitment
- Expect 10 hours per week per course
- Qualifier: 4 weeks of intensive study
- Can register for 1-4 courses per term based on availability

## Online Learning Flexibility
- All video lectures pre-recorded
- Accessible anytime on YouTube
- Can watch before joining to assess difficulty
- Learn at your own pace

## Exam Attendance Required
- Quizzes and exams must be attended in person
- Select exam cities convenient for you
- 2 quiz dates and 1 final exam per term per course

## Taking Breaks
- Can defer Foundation registration up to 3 terms after qualifier
- Once registered, course access for that term only
- Can rejoin in subsequent terms

## Coaching Not Required
No coaching needed. Self-study with provided materials is sufficient. IITM confirms program is designed for self-study.

## Past **Question** Papers
Available through student communities:
- gradedassignments.github.io/iit-madras-pyq
- quizpractice.space

## Study Groups
Student communities exist for peer support but program does not mandate group study.
