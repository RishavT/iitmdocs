Q142: If i miss this application, when can i apply again?
Answer: If you miss the application for the Qualifier Exam, you can apply again in the next term. Here are the key points regarding reapplication:

Unlimited Attempts: There are no limits on the number of times you can attempt the qualifier process in subsequent terms.

Full Fees: You will need to pay the full fees applicable for your category when you reapply.

Coursework Requirement: You must redo the 4 weeks of coursework and weekly graded assignments to become eligible for the qualifier exam again.

Important dates: https://study.iitm.ac.in/ds/admissions.html#AD0
