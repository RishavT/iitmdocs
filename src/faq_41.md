Q172: "What if I took an incomplete but the professor won't let me make up the exam?",Incomplete grade policies not in knowledge base,Incomplete Grade
Answer: Students who receive an Incomplete (I) grade in a course may opt to appear for a Make-Up Examination instead of repeating the course.
The Make-Up Examination must be registered for in the immediately subsequent term following the term in which the I grade was awarded.
If a student does not register for the Make-Up Examination in the subsequent term, the student will be required to repeat the entire course as a full course in the term in which they next apply.
