Q138: Is the degree awarded by IIT Madras valid worldwide?
Answer: Yes. The degree is awarded by IIT Madras, a nationally and internationally recognized institute, and is valid for employment and higher studies worldwide. It is as valid as the Btech awarded by IITM.
