# FAQs


**Question**: What is Paradox?
**Answer**: Paradox is an annual offline event hosted by the IIT Madras BS Degree program.
It is conducted once every year, usually in the months of May or June.



**Question**: When is Paradox conducted?
**Answer**: Paradox is typically conducted in late May to early June.

Examples mentioned:

* Paradox 2023: 25–28 May 2023
* Paradox 2024: 30 May – 2 June 2024
* Paradox 2025: 4–7 June 2025
* Paradox 2026: 9-14 June 2026



**Question**: Who can attend Paradox?
**Answer**: Students who have been issued a valid IIT Madras BS student ID can attend Paradox.

Qualifier-cleared students who have a valid student ID are also eligible to attend.



**Question**: Where can I find more details about Paradox?
**Answer**: More details about the Paradox event are available on the official Paradox website.
