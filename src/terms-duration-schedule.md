# Terms, Duration and Academic Schedule

## Terms Per Year
There are 3 terms in a year:
1. January Term (Jan-Apr)
2. May Term (May-Aug)
3. September Term (Sep-Dec)

## Term Structure
- Qualifier phase: 4 weeks of coursework + exam
- Regular term: Approximately 12-16 weeks including orientation and exams

## Qualifier Process Timeline
- Week 1-4: Online coursework and weekly assignments
- End of Week 4: First qualifier exam attempt
- End of Week 8: Second attempt (reattempt)

## After Passing Qualifier
- Attend orientation
- Complete Week 5-6 assignments
- Start regular Foundation term coursework

## Academic Schedule
The academic schedule including important dates is available at:
https://study.iitm.ac.in/ds/admissions.html#AD0

## Qualifier Result Validity
- Valid for 3 terms following the exam
- For Class 12 students: Valid for 3 terms from passing Class 12
- Can defer joining within validity period

## When Can I Join?
- January batch: Applications typically open Oct-Nov previous year
- May batch: Applications typically open Feb-Mar
- September batch: Applications typically open June-July

## Gap Between Registration and Term Start
There may be a gap between course registration deadline and term start to allow administrative processing and orientation scheduling.
