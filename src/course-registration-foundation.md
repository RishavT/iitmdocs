# Foundation Course Registration Process

## After Passing Qualifier
Course registration is NOT automatic. You must:
1. Log in to student portal
2. Access Course Registration Form
3. Select Foundation courses (up to 4 based on your score)
4. Upload Class 12 documentation if needed
5. Choose 2 exam cities
6. Pay course fees (~Rs 6000 per course)
7. Submit before deadline

## Registration Deadline
Typically 1-4 days after qualifier results. Check email and portal for exact dates.

## How Many Courses to Register
- Based on your qualifier score
- Maximum 4 courses per term
- JEE entrants: CCC (Credit Clearing Capability) of 4

## Foundation Level Courses
8 courses total in Foundation level:
- For Data Science: Mathematics, Statistics, English, Computational Thinking, Python, etc.
- Per course fee: Rs 6000 approximately

## Course Fees Payment
- Pay online through portal
- Cannot pay in person at campus
- Cannot pay by cheque
- No EMI option currently available
- Full fees must be paid before registration deadline

## Delaying Registration
- Can defer joining for up to 3 terms
- Qualifier result valid for 3 terms
- No need to redo qualifier within validity period

## Improvement Courses
If you have U grade (failed) in a subject like Math 1, you must:
- Re-register for that course in future term
- Complete all requirements again
- Pay course fee again
- Cannot take Math 2 if Math 1 has U grade
