Q145: After i clear the qualfier and wants to regsietr for the foundation level. Should I pay the whole fees for the foundation level ?
Answer: Foundation Level Fee Payment After Qualifier
Yes, after clearing the Qualifier exam, you will need to pay the fees for the Foundation level courses based on the number of courses you register for. Here are the details:

The fee for each Foundation course is approximately ₹6,000.
The total fees for the Foundation level depend on how many courses you choose to register for.
Here’s a breakdown of the fees based on the total credits:

Foundation only (32 credits): ₹48,000
Foundation + One Diploma (59 credits): ₹1,29,000
Foundation + Two Diplomas (86 credits): ₹2,10,000
BSc Degree (114 credits): ₹2,86,000 - ₹3,10,000
BS Degree (142 credits): ₹3,86,000 - ₹4,50,000
PG Diploma in AI & ML (162 credits): ₹4,86,000 - ₹5,90,000
MTech in AI & ML (182 credits): ₹6,86,000 - ₹7,90,000
Make sure to complete the payment before the registration deadline to secure your place in the courses.

Q146: can i come to campus to make the payment
Answer: No, you need not come to the campus to make the payment. All the payments are collected only online while you submit the application.
