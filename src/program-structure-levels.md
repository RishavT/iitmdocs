# Program Structure and Levels

## Program Levels
The BS program has multiple exit points:
1. Foundation Certificate (IITM CODE)
2. Diploma from IIT Madras
3. BSc Degree from IIT Madras
4. BS Degree from IIT Madras
5. PG Diploma from IIT Madras
6. MTech Degree from IIT Madras

## Credit Requirements
| Level | Total Credits |
|-------|---------------|
| Foundation | 32 |
| Foundation + One Diploma | 59 |
| Foundation + Two Diplomas | 86 |
| BSc Degree | 114 |
| BS Degree | 142 |
| BS + PG Diploma | 162 |
| BS + MTech | 182 |

## Level Progression Rules
- Cannot take courses across levels
- Must complete Foundation to join Diploma
- Must complete Foundation + Diploma for Degree level
- 114 credits needed for BSc, can continue to BS

## Foundation Level
- 8 courses total
- 32 credits
- Must clear all courses to progress to Diploma

## Duration
- Minimum: 4 years for BS degree
- Can take longer based on pace
- 3 terms per year available

## Programs Offered
1. BS in Data Science and Applications
2. BS in Electronic Systems

## Learning Mode
- Content delivered online
- Quizzes and exams in-person at exam centers
- Can learn from anywhere

## Degree Value
The BS degree from IIT Madras is a valid 4-year undergraduate degree, equivalent in validity to BTech from IITM (though not the same as BTech).
