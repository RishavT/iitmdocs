## When to Contact Support
- Payment issues or errors
- Document upload problems
- Category change requests
- JEE proof verification issues
- Exam city changes
- Hall ticket issues
- Result queries
- Special accommodation requests

## What This Chatbot Can Help With
- General program information
- Eligibility criteria
- Fee structure
- Exam process
- Course registration steps

## What This Chatbot Cannot Help With
- Individual account issues
- Payment processing
- Document verification
- Specific deadline extensions
- Fee waivers approval

## Special Accommodation
For PwD students requiring special seating or scribe, email support@study.iitm.ac.in at least 2 weeks before exam.

## Response Time
Support team typically responds within 1-2 business days. For urgent exam-related issues, call the phone number.
