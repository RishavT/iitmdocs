# IIT Madras BS Programme - Knowledge Base Summary

This document summarizes the topics and keywords available in the chatbot's knowledge base. Use this to understand what information exists and how users might ask about it.

---

## 1. ADMISSION & APPLICATION

**Keywords**: apply, application, admission, enroll, join, register, qualifier, eligibility, entry

### Topics Covered:
- **Qualifier Exam**: 4-week preparation, application fee Rs 3000 (General), fee waivers for SC/ST/PwD
- **Eligibility**: Class 12 passed, Math & English in Class 10, any background/age
- **JEE Entry**: JEE Advanced qualifiers can skip qualifier exam
- **Direct Admission to Diploma (DAD)**: For students with 2 years UG experience
- **Required Documents**: Photo, ID proof, category certificates
- **International Students**: Remote proctored exams, UAE exam centers

---

## 2. PROGRAMME STRUCTURE & LEVELS

**Keywords**: levels, structure, foundation, diploma, BSc, BS, degree, certificate, credits

### Four Levels:
1. **Foundation**: 8 courses, 32 credits - Certificate from CODE
2. **Diploma**: 12 courses + 4 projects, 54 credits - Diploma in Programming/Data Science
3. **BSc**: 28 credits (114 total) - BSc in Programming & Data Science
4. **BS**: 28 credits (142 total) - BS in Data Science and Applications

### Exit Points:
- Exit after Foundation with certificate
- Exit after Diploma(s)
- Exit after BSc degree
- Complete BS degree

## 4. FEES & PAYMENTS

**Keywords**: fee, cost, price, payment, waiver, scholarship, loan, afford

### Fee Structure:
- Foundation: Rs 32,000
- One Diploma: Rs 62,500
- Both Diplomas: Rs 1,25,000
- BSc Total: Rs 2,21,000 - 2,27,000
- BS Total: Rs 3,15,000 - 3,51,000

### Fee Waivers (based on category & income):
- SC/ST: 50-75% waiver
- PwD: 50-75% waiver
- EWS/OBC-NCL with income < 5 LPA: 50-75% waiver

### Other Fees:
- Credit transfer (NPTEL): Rs 1000/credit
- Re-registration (RKA): Rs 1000/term
- Readmission: Rs 2500

---

## 5. ASSESSMENTS & GRADING

**Keywords**: exam, quiz, assignment, OPPE, grade, marks, score, pass, fail, eligibility

### Assessment Types:
- Weekly online assignments (graded)
- Monthly in-person quizzes (Quiz 1 & 2)
- End-term exams (in-person)
- OPPE (Online Proctored Programming Exam) for coding courses
- Projects with viva

### Pass Criteria:
- Total score >= 50/100 to pass
- Assignment average >= 40/100 for exam eligibility
- Must attend at least 1 quiz

### Grading Scale:
- S (>=90), A (>=80), B (>=70), C (>=60), D (>=50)
- U = Fail, I = Incomplete

## 6. ACADEMIC CALENDAR & TERMS

**Keywords**: term, semester, calendar, schedule, deadline, registration, when, date

### Three Terms Per Year:
- January Term (Jan-Apr)
- May Term (May-Aug)
- September Term (Sep-Dec)

### Each Term:
- 12 weeks of coursework
- 2 quizzes + end-term exam
- Registration window before each term

---

## 7. ACADEMIC POLICIES & RULES

**Keywords**: policy, rule, repeat, drop, CCC, probation, struck off, LLM, plagiarism, honor code

### Key Policies:
- **CCC (Credit Clearing Capability)**: Max courses per term based on performance
- **Academic Probation**: 2 terms without registration
- **Struck Off**: 3 terms without registration
- **LLM/ChatGPT Usage**: Prohibited in assignments (plagiarism)
- **Course Repeat**: Must pay full fee again

### Completion Rules:
- Foundation: Complete 8 courses in max 9 terms
- Diploma: Complete in max 12 terms
- Time limits apply

## 9. HIGHER STUDIES & MASTERS

**Keywords**: masters, MTech, MS, PhD, GATE, research, upgrade, campus

### Pathways:
- **GATE Exam**: Apply to any IIT/NIT for MTech/MS/PhD
- **CFTI Entry**: CGPA >= 8.0 for MS at IIT Madras (no GATE required)
- **Project Staff Route**: Work at IITM, then apply for MS
- **On-Campus Upgrade**: Complete BS level on IITM campus
- **Online PG Diploma/MTech**: Continue after BS online
