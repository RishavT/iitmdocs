Q139: Can I write the exam from home and Are exam timings flexible?
Answer: No. All candidates who are present in India during the examination must write the exam in-person (offline) at exam centres allocated to them. Exam timings are fixed and usually follow IST. We conduct exams in about 180 cities across India. Check for the city closest to you and apply for that.

Q141: When will we have exams in a term?
Answer: Exam Schedule in a Term
Exams in a term for the IIT Madras BS program are typically scheduled as follows:

Quizzes:

There are usually two quizzes per term, with specific dates announced at the beginning of the term.
End Term Exam:

The end term exam is conducted at the conclusion of the term. The exact date will be provided in the academic calendar for that term.
For the most accurate and detailed schedule, including specific dates for quizzes and end term exams, please refer to the official academic calendar or announcements made at the start of each term.
