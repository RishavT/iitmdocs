# BS in Electronic Systems (ES) Program

## Program Overview
The BS in Electronic Systems is a four-year undergraduate program offered by IIT Madras alongside the BS in Data Science and Applications.

## Eligibility Requirements
In addition to general eligibility:
- Must have studied Physics and Mathematics in Class 12 or equivalent
- Class 10 Mathematics and English required

## Qualifier Subjects for ES
The Electronic Systems qualifier covers 4 subjects:
1. English I
2. Math for Electronics I
3. Electronic Systems Thinking and Circuits
4. Introduction to C Programming

## How to Register for BS Electronic Systems
1. Visit https://study.iitm.ac.in/es/
2. Click on Apply/Register
3. Complete application form
4. Pay application fee
5. Complete qualifier coursework
6. Pass qualifier exam

## Key Differences from Data Science
- Different qualifier subjects (includes Physics-based content)
- Different curriculum focused on electronic systems
- Same fee structure and program duration

## Cannot Switch Between Programs
- Once you clear qualifier for ES, you cannot switch to Data Science
- Once you clear qualifier for DS, you cannot switch to Electronic Systems
- Must reapply and redo qualifier for the other program
