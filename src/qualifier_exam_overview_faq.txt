Question: What exactly is the qualifier exam?
Answer: The qualifier exam is a 4-hour exam conducted after 4 weeks of online classes and assignments.
It tests only the content taught during those 4 weeks.
Clearing the qualifier with the required cut-offs makes a learner eligible to register for Foundation-level courses in the IIT Madras BS program.



Question: Why is the qualifier exam required?
Answer: The qualifier exam is mandatory for regular entry applicants.
It ensures that learners meet the minimum academic readiness required to enter the Foundation level of the program.



Question: Is the qualifier exam the same for all programs?
Answer: No.
The syllabus differs by program:

* BS in Data Science and Applications: English 1, Maths-1, Statistics-1, Computational Thinking
* BS in Electronic Systems: English I, Math for Electronics I, Electronic Systems Thinking and Circuits, Introduction to C Programming



Question: Is the qualifier exam competitive or only qualifying?
Answer: The qualifier exam is primarily a qualifying exam, not a competitive one.
Candidates must meet minimum subject-wise and overall cut-offs to pass.



Question: Does clearing the qualifier guarantee admission?
Answer: Yes.
Clearing the qualifier guarantees admission to the Foundation level provided there are no documentation issues.



Question: Who conducts the qualifier exam?
Answer: The qualifier exam is conducted by the IIT Madras BS program administration.



Question: Is Mathematics compulsory to attempt the qualifier?
Answer: Mathematics is not compulsory as an advanced school subject.
The program is designed to be inclusive, and the qualifier tests only what is taught during the 4-week coursework, regardless of prior background.



Question: Is the qualifier easier or harder than end-term exams?
Answer: The qualifier is generally considered moderately difficult but easier than regular term exams, provided the learner stays consistent with lectures and assignments.



Question: How much time should I prepare for the qualifier?
Answer: The qualifier spans 4 weeks of coursework, with an expected engagement of about 10 hours per week per course.
Consistent daily preparation is recommended.



Question: Is coaching required to clear the qualifier?
Answer: No.
Coaching is not required. Learners can clear the qualifier through self-study using lectures, assignments, and practice material provided by IIT Madras.



Question: Can I clear the qualifier with self-study?
Answer: Yes.
The qualifier is fully based on the 4 weeks of provided content, and many students clear it through consistent self-study.
