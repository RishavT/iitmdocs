Q114: Can I give my exams at the IIT Madras campus if I live in Chennai?
Answer: No, the IIT Madras campus is NOT an official exam center for the BS program. However, Chennai IS available as an exam city. You can select Chennai as one of your two exam cities during course registration. Exams are conducted at designated exam centers in Chennai, not at the IIT Madras campus itself. Q115: Is Chennai available as an exam city for quizzes and exams?
Answer: Yes, Chennai is one of the available exam cities for quizzes and exams in the IIT Madras BS Program. You can select Chennai as one of your two preferred exam cities during course registration. Note that exam centers in Chennai are at designated venues, not at the IIT Madras campus.

Q116: Where exactly are exams conducted in Chennai?
Answer: Exams in Chennai are conducted at designated exam centers, not at the IIT Madras campus. The specific exam center location will be communicated when you book your exam slot.
