# Content

## Official communication channels

All official communication related to the Qualifier Process including assignment deadlines, examination schedules, hall ticket release, and publication of results will be shared with learners through the following channels: The registered email address of the learner; WhatsApp notifications sent to the registered mobile number; Announcements displayed on the student portal

Learners are advised to check their registered email frequently and ensure that WhatsApp notifications remain enabled so that no important information or deadline is missed.

## Marks, results, and alerts

The qualifier exam marks will be displayed within the login on the portal along with an email and whatsapp alert sent to the candidates about this.

Learners who qualify will be permitted to proceed with further registration, and an Admission Letter will be generated for each eligible candidate.

If the proof is found to be valid: They will be issued the admission letter.

This re-attempt application form will open up immediately after the qualifier results are announced.

Once the qualifier result is declared, you can register for foundational courses and continue with the program.
