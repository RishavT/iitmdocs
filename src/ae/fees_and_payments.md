# Content

## Qualifier Exam application fees

The application fee for the Qualifier exam must be paid during registration and is non-refundable under any circumstances.

General Category / OBC Applicant: ₹4000

SC / ST Category / PwD (≥ 40% disability) Applicant: ₹2000

SC / ST Category Applicant who is ALSO PwD (≥ 40% disability): ₹1000

Please note that the Qualifier Examination fee is non-refundable under all circumstances, including cases where the learner misses the examination or does not pass it.

## Re-attempt fees

General Category / OBC Applicant: ₹2000 re-attempt fee

SC / ST Category / PwD (>= 40% disability) Applicant: ₹1000 re-attempt fee

SC / ST Category Applicant who is ALSO PwD (>= 40% disability): ₹500 re-attempt fee

## Later term retry fees

They have to pay the full fees as per the fee norms applicable for their category. (and additional exam facilitation fee in case of candidates outside India)

## Course payment

Learners pay only for the number of credits they are signing up for (online payment only).
