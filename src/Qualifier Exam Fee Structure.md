# Qualifier Exam Fee Structure

The application fee for the Qualifier exam must be paid during registration and is non-refundable under any circumstances.

## Application Fee by Category

| Category | Application Fee |
|----------|-----------------|
| General Category / OBC Applicant | Four thousand rupees (Rs. 4000) |
| SC / ST Category / PwD (40% or more disability) Applicant | Two thousand rupees (Rs. 2000) |
| SC / ST Category Applicant who is ALSO PwD (40% or more disability) | One thousand rupees (Rs. 1000) |

## Re-attempt Fee (Within Same Term)

For candidates who were absent or failed the first attempt and want to re-attempt within the same term:

| Category | Re-attempt Fee |
|----------|----------------|
| General Category / OBC Applicant | Two thousand rupees (Rs. 2000) |
| SC / ST Category / PwD (40% or more disability) Applicant | One thousand rupees (Rs. 1000) |
| SC / ST Category Applicant who is ALSO PwD (40% or more disability) | Five hundred rupees (Rs. 500) |

## Important Notes

- The Qualifier Examination fee is non-refundable under all circumstances
- This includes cases where the learner misses the examination or does not pass it
- Candidates outside India may have to pay an additional exam facilitation fee
