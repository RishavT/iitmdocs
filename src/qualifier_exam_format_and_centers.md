# Exam Cities for IIT Madras BS Program

Quizzes and exams for the IIT Madras BS Program are conducted in person at exam centres across India and abroad.

## Selecting Exam Cities

- Students pick 2 cities among the choices provided for their quizzes and exams for each term
- Students have the freedom to choose a different set of cities than the ones they opted for in previous terms
- Students can choose a different set of cities for every quiz/final exam while registering for courses
- Booking will be done with the Exam vendor for slots in these selected cities

- City availability may vary by term
- Students should select cities convenient for them during course registration

# Exam Format and Proctoring

## Qualifier Exam Format
- Duration: 4 hours (240 minutes)
- Mode: In-person at exam center (India) / Online proctored (outside India)
- Type: MCQ, MSQ, numerical, and short **Answer** **Question**s
- Subjects: All 4 subjects tested on same day
- No negative marking

## Exam Mode by Location
- Students in India: MUST write at exam center (in-person, offline)
- Students outside India: Remote proctored exam from residence

## No Online Exam for India Residents
All students residing in India or physically present in India on exam day MUST write exam at exam center. NO exceptions for medical emergencies.

## Calculator
No physical calculator needed. Calculator enabled in exam window.

## Open Book or Closed Book
Closed book exam. In-person, proctored, invigilated.

## Documents Required at Exam Center
- Hall ticket (physical copy mandatory)
- Valid ID proof (Aadhaar/PAN/Passport/College ID)

## Aadhaar Mandatory?
No, Aadhaar is not mandatory. Any valid ID proof accepted: Aadhaar, PAN card, Passport, or College ID.

## Quiz and End-Term Exams
- Similar format to qualifier
- Must attend at least one of two quizzes to be eligible for final exam
- Exams conducted at selected exam cities

## Exam Cities
- Select 2 cities during registration
- Can change cities during edit window
